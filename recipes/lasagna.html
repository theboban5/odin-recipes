Zucchini Lasagna

Description

Out of gluten free pasta? You must have lots of zucchini! Make Zucchini Lasagna.

It's good, AND good for you

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil
  • Bechamel sauce

Steps

  1. cut zucchini
  2. put tomato sauce and zucchinis in a dish
  3. top w/ cheese, sauces, cheese -> keep layering
  4. bake

It's really that easy!
