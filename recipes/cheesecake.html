Ube (Purple Sweet Potato) Cheesecake

Description

This is the perfect sweet treat or dessert for a special occasion. The purple color gives it that wow-factor that will definitely wow

Ingredients

Crust

  • graham cracker crumbs
  • butter
  • sugar
  • salt

Filling

  • cream cheese
  • whipped cream
  • eggs
  • salt
  • vanilla extract
  • ube jam & ube extract

Steps

  1. Crust: stir grahams, sugar, butter + salt, put in pan, bake!
  2. Filling: add sugar to cream cheese, add ube jam, add eggs, add ube + vanilla extract (beat b/w steps)
  3. Combine!: Pour filling onto crust, put back in the oven, bake until center is almost set
