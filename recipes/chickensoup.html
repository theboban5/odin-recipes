Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

A chicken noodle soup recipe that's a very easy, very good substitute for when you don't have time to make your soup totally from scratch.

You'll find the full recipe below, but here is a tip, remember this is a classic recipe.

Ingredients

  • Onions and Celery
  • Broth
  • Chicken (of course)
  • Carrots
  • Basil and (or?) Oregano

Steps

  1. Melt the butter, add onion & celery, cook it 5 minutes or so
  2. Add everything else - stir it in and boil
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes (or so)
